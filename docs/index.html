Mads Stoumann - Welcome

Mads Stoumann

Hi, I'm Mads.
Thanks for stopping by! I'm a web developer, graphic designer, type designer, musician, food lover, comicbook-geek … as well as husband and father, located just south of Copenhagen, Denmark.

I work as a Technical Director at Valtech.
Get in touch:

Graphic Design

As a graphic designer, I've been lettering, coloring, layouting and designing hundreds and hundreds of comics, books and magazines. I've designed the typefaces used for the Nordic Spirou- and Gaston Lagaffe-comics, created ads, logos, brochures, toy-wrappings … and much more.

With a group of friends, I co-founded what is today Denmarks largest comicbook-publisher: Forlaget Zoom.

Pro­gramming

I've worked quite a lot with relational databases and the T-SQL-language. My backend-skills are limited to Visual Basic — I definitely feel more comfortable with JavaScript these days!

For the Danish Medicines Agency, I've developed two applications: One for tracking the storage-location of specific medicine applications, and one for calculating patterns in symptoms for vaccines.
